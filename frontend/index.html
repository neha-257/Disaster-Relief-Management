About Disaster Relief Management

Established 2025

Our Mission

Safe Horizon is a Disaster Relief Management System that is designed to streamline and optimize disaster response operations, ensuring that resources are efficiently allocated and victims receive timely assistance.

  • Centralized management of all disaster relief operations
  • Efficient tracking of victims, resources, and volunteers
  • Quick response to emergency situations with minimal delays

Our system helps disaster management teams coordinate relief efforts, track victims and missing persons, manage relief camps, handle donations, monitor inventory, and coordinate volunteers—all from a single integrated platform.
